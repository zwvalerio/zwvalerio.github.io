OUR SERVICES

Alert Warnings and Alternative Route Suggestions

Since Malolos is a busy city, it is no surprise that the roads are always congested with public and private vehicles. Additionally, it has low lands, which is prone to floodings. As a solution, MalRoute will warn people and will also give alternative routes to to drivers and some commuters.

Locate Places

MalRoute also offers help to new settlers and tourists in Malolos City. Our service includes helping people locate places where to eat, shop, etc.

Traffic Rules and Signs

Sometimes, drivers and commuters add to the burden of traffic because they do not follow proper road and traffic rules. As an action to this, MalRoute will also remind the public to follow the rules so traffic would be lessened.
